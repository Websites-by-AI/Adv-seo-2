## Verification checklist before contracting

1. Confirm legal identity, invoice details and authorized signatory.
2. Request at least two recent client references.
3. Ask for healthcare/medical portfolio evidence and written permission to show it.
4. Require ownership of Search Console, Analytics, domain, hosting and content assets to remain with the client.
5. Ask for an itemized 90-day plan, SLA and reporting definitions.
6. Do not accept a guarantee of rank 1.
7. Require medical review responsibility to be defined in the contract.
8. Confirm link-building sources, media budget and direct invoices.
9. Run a paid pilot or technical discovery phase before a long contract.
10. Upload authorized high-resolution logos if they will appear in proposals; favicon URLs in the app are identification fallbacks only.
